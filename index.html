Digital Electronics

Welcome to Digital Electronics

Digital electronics is a branch of electronics that deals with digital signals and circuits. This website provides simulations and explanations for various digital electronics concepts, including adders, subtractors, flip flops, and logic gates.

Adders

Adders are digital circuits that perform addition operations. They are used in various digital systems, including computers and calculators.

Subtractors

Subtractors are digital circuits that perform subtraction operations. They are used in various digital systems, including computers and calculators.

Flip Flops

Flip flops are digital circuits that store binary data. They are used in various digital systems, including computers and memory devices.

Logic Gates

Logic gates are digital circuits that perform logical operations. They are used in various digital systems, including computers and calculators.
